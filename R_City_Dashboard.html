Shiny for User-Centric Digital Public Sector

ArgosMs

March 28, 2019

I bumped into this dashboard and found it pretty cool: a nice building block towards improving local governance. Dashboards like this help officials and citizens share information on demand. And better communication inevitable leads to better decisions, planning and policies. Thus I decided to play around a bit with Shiny myself to see what it feels like.

It took me a bit of time to wrap my head around the structure of this package and its main arguments. But the more I read (thanks everyone!) and mistakes I made, the more comfortable I started feeling, which them led me to quietly begin cheering my babysteps.

The outcome of this exercise is still very afar from all the possibilities that Shiny offers. However, I have really enjoyed this initial experiment mainly because this is the first app I created without knowing anything about html and also because I now share a bit of that satisfaction that my fellow webdesigners enjoy when building these cool things that get so much of our daily attention.
